Women in Tech Allies

We're Moving IT Forward, Together

Women in Tech Allies is a group to advocate for diversity in technology through networking, tech talks, mentorship, etc. We look for opportunities to support our current peers in tech, women attempting to transition into tech from other backgrounds, as well as helping our youth grow into future tech leaders.

We believe that change comes from within. For the technology and developer community to become more inclusive and accessible for women and other under-represented demographics, those already within the community must be part of that change.

The Women in Tech Allies group is a space to apply your interests, expertise, experience, knowledge, support and encouragement to promote new tech talent. We encourage all genders, races, and backgrounds to join our meetup and the tech community.

MEETUPS

Women in Tech Allies holds monthly meetups that offer opportunities to learn new tools and technologies and build professional skills.  WITA is dedicated to providing free resources to help women transition into and succeed in information technology careers through networking and education.
